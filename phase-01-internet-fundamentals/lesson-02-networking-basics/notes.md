# Notes — Lesson 2

## Key Ideas

- A network is a group of connected devices.
- An ISP connects a local network to the Internet.
- A router connects different networks and forwards packets.
- A switch connects devices inside the same LAN.
- Private IPs are used inside local networks.
- Public IPs are reachable from the Internet.
- NAT translates private IPs to public IPs and back.

## Production Note

Most home and office networks use one public IP for many private devices through NAT/PAT.
